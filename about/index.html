About me

Introduction

Hello again! My name is Ryan Pham. I'm wrapping up my last year of the Bachelor of Design at the University of Melbourne, where I am majoring in User Experience Design and Computer Science. I am excited to explore more of design world outside of university, and I am especially interested in clean and accessible design systems that consider all walks of life.

I aspire to be a Product Designer or Creative Technologist within the social innovation sector. I love to tell stories and help others to share theirs as well. To me, design is about communication and expression. It feels so incredibly fulfilling to craft the vehicle that carries a conversation. I hope that my work will lead to a more equitable and empathetic world.

When I'm not designing or coding, I love to think and to write. I currently publish a newsletter that expresses how I view my microcosm of the world.
