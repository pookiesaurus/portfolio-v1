About me

Introduction

Hello again! My name is Ryan Pham. I'm wrapping up my last year of the Bachelor of Design at the University of Melbourne, where I am majoring in User Experience Design and Computer Science. I am excited to explore more of design world outside of university, and I am especially interested in clean and accessible design systems that consider all walks of life.

After graduating, I aspire to be a Product Designer within the Social Innovation sector. I find that my passion for a better world and my love of design go hand in hand towards this cause. To me, design is about communication and expression. Therefore, it is fitting that I love to tell stories and to help others to share theirs as well. It feels so incredibly fulfilling to craft the vehicle that carries a conversation, and I hope that my work will help spread messages and demand action that leads to a more equitable and empathetic world.

When I'm not designing or coding, I love to think and to write. I currently publish a newsletter that expresses how I view my microcosm of the world.
